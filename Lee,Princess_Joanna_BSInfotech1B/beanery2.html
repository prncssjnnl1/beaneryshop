Products

Enjoy Fresh Brewed Coffee and Sweet Foods at The Beanery!

MILKSHAKE

Milkshake is a sweet, cold and frothy beverage typically made from milk, ice cream, and flavorings such as fruit, chocolate or caramel syrup.

DONUTS

Donuts are a type of sweet fried pastry, typically in the shape of a ring, and often topped with glaze or sprinkles.

MILKTEA

Milk tea is a popular beverage made from tea, milk, and sugar, often served with tapioca pearls or other flavored jelly-like toppings.

MACARONS

Macarons are a type of sweet French pastry made from ground almonds and meringue, sandwiched together with a variety of flavored fillings.

About Us

The Beanery, we aspire for quality, character and excellence in all we do.

Product We aim to serve the best quality and most interesting varieties of coffees, teas, espresso drinks and accompaniments at all times to everyone.
Service We strive to treat our customers respectfully as our guests; we intend to offer our guests service that is prompt, efficient, friendly, and relaxed.
Atmosphere We will provide a pleasant, welcoming environment, and will maintain a clean and attractive coffee house at all times.
Work Environment We want The Beanery to be a great place to work. We aim to create a positive, respectful workplace and to empower our staff to be themselves and to achieve their fullest potential.
Community The Beanery is committed to furthering the spirit and well-being of our neighborhood and the overall Cambridge community.
