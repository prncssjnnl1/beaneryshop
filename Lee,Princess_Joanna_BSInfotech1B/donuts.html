Donut

Donuts are a type of pastry typically made with dough that is deep-fried, giving them a crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior. They are often round with a hole in the center, but can also come in various shapes and sizes. Donuts can be coated with sugar, glaze, frosting, or sprinkles, and can be filled with jam, custard, or cream.
